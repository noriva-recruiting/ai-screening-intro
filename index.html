Meet NORVO™ – Your Digital Evaluator

NORVO™ is NORVIA's proprietary AI-based screening assistant. It reviews candidates in the early hiring stage and decides whether they make it to the next stage.

Trained on over 50,000 interviews, NORVO™ analyzes what you say and how you say it – looking for signs of competence, confidence, and fit with NORVIA’s values.

During your interview, NORVO™ continuously evaluates:

  • Your answers – strengths, motivation, and fit for NORVIA
  • Your communication – fluency, tone, pacing
  • Your behavior – eye contact, expression, posture
  • Your presence – confidence and authenticity

What to Expect

Your AI interview with NORVO™ consists of two parts:

  1. Self-introduction (1 minute)
    Please pitch yourself freely and authentically to NORVO™. This is your opportunity to showcase your background, motivation, and personal strengths.
  2. Spontaneous interview questions (3 × 1 minute)
    Answer spontaneously and honestly.

You may complete the interview in German or English; NORVO™ will process either language.

  • During the interview, your video and audio will be recorded and analyzed in real time by NORVO™.
